## ✨ FEATURES

### 🧭 Auto Navigation
- Optimized local navigation system (runtime baking)  
- Automatically finds path to nearest saddle point  

---

### 🪑 Saddle Placements
- Dynamic left/right mounting based on position  
- Smooth transition from side to center  

---

### 🪢 Rein Rope Adjustments
- Physics-based rein movement  
- Adjustable stiffness and drag settings  

---

### 🔊 Dynamic Foot Audio
- Footstep sounds change based on surface (grass, dirt, etc.)  

---

### 🔊 Base Audio
- Horse sounds (whinny, snort)  
- Animation-timed audio playback  

---

### 🏔️ Slope Adjustments
- Horse and rider adapt to uneven terrain  
- Natural tilt and alignment on slopes  

---

### 🦵 IK & Hoof Placement
- Foot IK for accurate ground placement  
- Hoof direction correction using LookAt system  

---

### 🐎 Neck & Head Movement
- Neck IK reacts to terrain slope  
- Rider head follows camera direction  

---

### 🎥 Camera States
- Integrated third-person camera system  
- Different states (idle, walk, run, mounted)  

---

### 🎞️ Animations
- Idle, walk, and movement animations  
- Whinny and idle variations  

---

### 💨 VFX & Effects
- Dust particles during movement  
- Decals for added realism  

---

### 🐎 Movement States
- Multiple horse movement modes (walk, jog)  

---

### ⬇️ Smart Dismount System
- Detects safe side (left/right) before dismount  
- Prevents dismount when blocked  

---

### 📘 Dedicated Documentation
- Full breakdown of system structure and logic  

---

### ⚙️ Additional Details
- Animation-driven mounting system  
- Smooth rider–horse synchronization  
- Optimized update flow  

---
